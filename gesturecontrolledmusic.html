Kinect DJ

An experience of producing music using body gestures

Introduction

What is this about

This project is an interactive experience that allows user to produce music with their body movements. This is a new way to engage audience through DJing wherein the artist himself involves with the music interaction through body movements. This was done as part of Techno-entertainment show conducted by the students of IIT Madras that is displayed in front of 5000 audience.

Approach

How is it achieved

This is a three step process. First we used the skeletal tracking functionality of Kinect sensor to detect the human movement. In the second step we used feed the input from the kinect sensor to Unity game engine where the visual interactions are made. The final step is the music production which is done on Ableton Live and Virtual DJ softwares that are linked to Unity. The skeletal tracking is the input, Unity is the interface and music is the output. We created various features ranging from playing simple musical instruments to creating complete music with various loops and finally tweaking the music with effects.
