GLOCAL

Connecting global businesses to local influencers

Introduction

What is this about

This project is a product challenge posed by Pitney Bowes company and was done in collaboration with Cornell Tech. Me along with four other students of Cornell Tech worked on this project. The challenge was How might we help cross-border brands introduce their products to consumers who live in different countries?

Approach

How did we arrive at the solution

At first we wanted to understand the current cross-border e-commerce scenario. So, we conducted a survey among a group of young individuals to find out what is that they are looking for in an international e-commerce. We found out that people are looking for brands that they can trust with quality as nowadays there are a lot of counterfiet products in the market. Also people are looking for faster shipping with lower costs.

Solution

What did we come up with

Considering the product challenge and the survey outcomes we came up with a solution that can connect businesses who want to go international to local influencers of the target country inroder to boost the sales and increase the trust among consumers. Our product is named as GLOCAL which is essentially bridging gap between international brands and local influencers.

Risks

Challenges of doing this product

When building a product for the real world there are always risks associated with them, Similarly we identified certain risks with our product and tried to test for these risks and understand how the consumers would respond to such a product. Our utmost primary concern was whether Local influencers would subscribe to our service. To test this aspect of our product we developed a fake website briefing our service and sent out a large number of people through social media. Within a week we had 1532 number of clicks and 146 subscribers, a conversion rate of nearly 10%. We analyzed the results of this experiment and incorporated the necessary changes in our mobile app designs.

Final product

Functionality and the look and feel

The concept here is to make a matching like platform to connect businesses with local influencers. In order to give leverage to both the participants in choosing whom they want to work with, we decided to use matching algorithm.

As there is a tendancy for more influencers to join the service and business are provided with hefty lot of influencers we further shortened down the burden to the businesses and created a search algorithm to bring down the number of influncers a company can see. This takes in the criteria posed by the business and matches with the influencers directly.
